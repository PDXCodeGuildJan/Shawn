Hi, Shawn here. I climbed a literal corporate ladder for the last decade, and reached a point in my former trade, carpentry, where interesting new challenges were few. So now I'm making a career change to software development. I graduate from PDX Code Guild in April of 2016. I have experience relevant to the field and a strong academic background in math. For a year when housing slowed way down, I worked on contract as an underwriter for Kaiser Permanente's Small Business Group. I enjoyed learning their huge arcane database 'CM', and I initiated automating testing tasks in Visual Basic and SQL.

I am exploring what special areas exist where I can make the biggest contribution. Programming is fun, and it is the closest a common fellow can come to being a wizard in 2016. I get fired up about the language Python. It has a great balance between doing your housekeeping automagically, but still it allows you to get down to controlling very fine details.
